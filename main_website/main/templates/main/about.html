About Us

We are a team of five passionate college students who have developed a Topic Modelling Website. Our goal is to make topic modelling accessible and easy for everyone, regardless of their technical background or expertise.

Our team comprises students from similar academic backgrounds, which is computer science.We have leveraged our individual skills and expertise to create a website that is user-friendly, efficient, and accurate.

Our website offers a range of features that enable users to analyze and visualize their text data easily. Our user-friendly interface allows users to upload their text data, select the parameters for analysis, and visualize the results in an intuitive manner.

We have incorporated several topic modelling algorithms, including LDA and RAKE, to ensure that users have access to a range of options to suit their needs. Our algorithms have been fine-tuned and tested to ensure accuracy and efficiency in topic modelling.

At the heart of our project is the belief that topic modelling can be a powerful tool for researchers, businesses, and individuals to gain insights from their text data. Our website offers an easy-to-use platform to achieve this goal.

We are excited about the potential of our project and are committed to continually improving and expanding our website to meet the needs of our users. We believe that our website can help individuals and organizations make sense of their text data and unlock valuable insights that can drive growth and innovation.
